Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Broccoli Row respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through this website and how we use it. It is placeholder content for demonstration purposes and should be reviewed before going live.

1. What we collect

We only collect information you choose to give us. If you send private feedback through this site, we collect your name, email address, star rating and comments. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising pixels on this website.

2. How we use it

We use the details you provide to read and respond to your feedback and to improve the experience at Broccoli Row. We do not sell or rent your information to anyone.

3. How feedback is handled

The private feedback form is delivered to us by a third-party form service (FormSubmit) purely to pass your message to our inbox. Your message is sent to the business and is not published publicly.

4. Keeping information safe

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you share and keep it only as long as needed to respond to you and keep our own records.

5. Your rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020 you can ask to see the information we hold about you and request that we correct it. To make a request, get in touch using the details below.

6. Contact

Questions about your privacy? Call us on 03 548 9621 or email hello@broccolirow.co.nz.
